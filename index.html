The lung that grew

For more than a decade, Milan has consistently cultivated its green space. Each year, more square meters for each inhabitant. A silent bet that today totals almost 26 million square meters.

Total Evolution vs Per Capita (2011-2023)
Chapter 2 of 6

Consume less, live the same

Between 2000 and 2011, Milan drastically reduced its gas and water consumption per inhabitant. Electricity remained stable. Green space grew. The city demonstrated that efficiency and quality of life coexist.

Methane gas was the resource with the greatest transformation: −131 m³ per inhabitant in just 11 years. Equivalent to thousands of tons less CO₂ emitted. The city began its decarbonization long before it was a trend.
